# Background Music

Place chapter background music files here:

- **ch1-theme.mp3** - Chapter 1 background music
- **ch2-theme.mp3** - Chapter 2 background music
- **ch3-theme.mp3** - Chapter 3 background music
- **ch4-theme.mp3** - Chapter 4 background music

## Audio Specifications

- **Format**: MP3 or OGG
- **Style**: MSX-style chiptune/8-bit music
- **Loop**: Files should loop seamlessly
- **Volume**: Will be set to 70% by default (adjustable in scenes.json)

## Optional

If no music file is found, the scenes will play without background music.
